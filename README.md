# Promote and Boost Short Film Reach as well as establish the presence of the shortfilm production house (makeamovieproductions)

### Mission Statement: Making a film needs nobody’s permission, it needs initiation. We took the initiative and started making something that we like and something we would like to see on the big screen. Sometimes we just need to start, just make a movie and learn on the go.

- Social media presence, tailored posts based on platforms (twitter,instagram,youtube)
- Twitter is to share updates about the making of the short film and once the short film is released, thank the involved technicians, etc
- Instagram to share making videos, the music composition process, the original OST track (find out how to upload on spotify/youtube), make a carousel post of the making of the short film (pre-production, production, post-production) documentation
- Youtube to post the short film, collect feedback from comments, find out the response of the audience and understand the short film performance based on analytics of the video, post the OST of the short-film separately; make a long form video explaining the BTS process (from the director)
- Keep making YT shorts/Insta reels that are engaging and explain the making of a shortfilm and the hurdles
