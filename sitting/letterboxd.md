## 4. Media Assets (Requirements)
Make sure your files meet these specific TMDb standards:

- Poster: Ratio: 2:3 (e.g., 2000 x 3000 px)
- Rule: No "Coming Soon" text or website URLs. Must be a clean movie poster.
- Backdrop (Fanart): Ratio: 16:9 (e.g., 3840 x 2160 px)
- Rule: High quality, no text (textless version of a key scene).
- Videos: YouTube/Vimeo Link: (Trailer, Teaser, or Clip)

## 5. Content Score Checklist
To get a high "Content Score" (which makes your movie show up higher in search results), ensure you have:

- [ ] At least 1 Poster
- [ ] At least 1 Backdrop
- [ ] Full Release Date
- [ ] Detailed Overview (at least 50 words)
- [ ] At least 5 Cast Members
- [ ] At least 3 Key Crew Members (Director, Writer, Producer)
